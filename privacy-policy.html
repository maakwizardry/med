Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 7, 2026

1. Introduction

Medtime RCM ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and protected health information (PHI). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our medical billing and coding services.

2. HIPAA Compliance

As a provider of Revenue Cycle Management services, we are a Business Associate under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). We strictly adhere to all HIPAA Privacy and Security Rules regarding the protection, handling, and transmission of PHI. We have implemented robust physical, technical, and administrative safeguards to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of all PHI entrust to us.

3. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, practice name, and job title when you voluntarily provide it through our contact forms.
  • Protected Health Information (PHI): Patient demographic data, insurance information, medical codes, and billing records provided by covered entities for the purpose of billing services.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you access and use our website, including IP address, browser type, and operating system.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our RCM services.
  • Process claims, manage denials, and handle appeals.
  • Communicate with you regarding your account or inquiries.
  • Improve our website and services.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

5. Data Security

We utilize industry-standard security measures, including encryption (SSL/TLS), firewalls, secure servers, and access controls, to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Disclosure to Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information or PHI to third parties. We may disclose information to:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist us in operating our business (e.g., clearinghouses, cloud storage), bound by strict confidentiality agreements and BAAs.
  • Legal Requirements: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete your information. To exercise these rights or for any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us using the information below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
