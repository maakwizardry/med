Our Services

A complete suite of revenue cycle management solutions designed to improve your practice's financial health, from patient intake to final payment.

Unmatched Medical Coding Accuracy

Our certified coders (CPC, AHIMA) ensure that every diagnosis and procedure is coded accurately to the highest specificity. We stay up-to-date with the latest ICD-10, CPT, and HCPCS changes to prevent denials before they happen.

  • Audit-ready documentation and compliance checks.
  • Specialty-specific coding expertise.
  • Regular feedback loops to improve documentation.

Fast & Clean Claims Submission

We scrub every claim for errors prior to submission, resulting in a 99% clean claim rate. Whether electronic or paper, we ensure your claims reach payers quickly for faster reimbursement.

  • Daily submission of claims within 24-48 hours.
  • Electronic clearinghouse integration.
  • Immediate correction of front-end rejections.

Aggressive Denial Management

Don't let denials eat into your revenue. We analyze the root cause of every denial and launch strategic appeals to recover what you are owed.

  • Root cause analysis to prevent recurrence.
  • Timely filing of appeals.
  • Detailed denial reporting by payer and reason.

Proactive A/R Follow-Up

We don't just bill; we collect. Our team actively monitors aging accounts and contacts payers to resolve outstanding balances, significantly reducing your days in A/R.

  • Systematic follow-up on all claims > 30 days.
  • Patient statement management.
  • Insurance verification and eligibility checks.

Streamlined Provider Credentialing

Getting empanelled with payers is critical for your revenue. We handle the tedious paperwork for new enrollments and re-credentialing, ensuring you stay in-network and get paid.

  • End-to-end enrollment application management.
  • CAQH profile setup and maintenance.
  • Tracking of re-validation dates to prevent lapses.
