Our Story

Partners in Your Financial Success

At Medtime RCM, we believe that healthcare providers should focus on what they do best: patient care. The complexities of medical billing, coding, and insurance follow-ups can divert valuable time and resources away from clinical work.

Founded by a team of healthcare and finance veterans, we set out to build a revenue cycle management solution that is transparent, efficient, and technology-driven. Our goal is simple: to help medical practices thrive by ensuring they get paid fairly and quickly for their services.
